MOODi

MOODi is a new medical application for people that suffer from different forms of depression and mental illnesses. It allows patients to create different types of activities for themselves and tracks their mood before and after. MOODI generates graphs and statistics for the patient and the GP and helps see what activities positively affect their mood providing help along the way.

Activities

Activities are what make this app. You can create activities with no restrictions. They can be anything you want and there is no category for it. You'll always be notified when an activity is coming up, this is where you will rate how you feel currently. Then when you've done the activity you simply go back and mark it as complete! and don't worry, if you miss an activity we got your back!

Dashboard

Your dashboard will hold all of the statistics gathered from your activities. It has been designed to provide you with accurate yet minimalistic data to let you know where you are and where you are going. We'll track your information for you so that you can see your daily improvements in a matter of seconds.

Calendar

All of your activities will be recorded on your calendar. So if you ever forget what's coming up or whats going on you can simply pop your calendar open and remind yourself!
